intro
Gorrill House, situated four blocks south of the UC campus at 2727 Dwight Way, is a 12-bedroom, 5-bath coed residence offering accommodation in private or shared rooms. The house is a beautiful historic landmark building that has been converted to a private residence hall.
Amenities include:
  • High Speed Internet in all bedrooms and common areas.
  • Study equipped with a network laser printer.
  • Attractively furnished Dining Room, Study, Sitting Room, and Living Room with piano and HDTV.
  • Bedrooms furnished with hardwood desks, dressers, chests, bookcases, beds, reading lamps, full length mirrors, and area rugs on hardwood floors.
  • Kitchen fully equipped with small appliances, cooking utensils, dishes, ect. Individual locks on food storage cupboards in Kitchen and adjoining Pantry.
  • Free washer and dryer. Iron, ironing board and vacuum provided.
  • Weekly maid service for all common areas.
  • DSL, HDTV cable service and all utilities except telephone service included in fee.
  • Individually keyed locks on all bedroom doors. Secure indoor bicycle storage.
  • Complete renovation including fully engineered seismic upgrade, fire sprinkler system and solar electric and hot water systems.
  • Resident Assistant in residence.
Location

Campus

The Gorill House is within 5 minutes walking distance to campus and right down the block from the Fall Program for Freshman facility. It is located on the South side of the school.

Food Courts

Restaurants and snack shops are all a few blocks away from the G-House. Visit the popular Asian Ghetto or eat all along Telegraph!

Transportation

Right around the corner is your nearest bus stop. If you've got your bus pass, you're set to go out of town at your convinience.

Floor Plans

Lower Floor

The lower level of G-House fields 2 doubles and 3 singles. It provides two bathrooms to accomodate these rooms, as well as access to the garage and the laundry. Lights automatically turn on to greet visitors to the hallway.

Ground Floor

The main floor of the G-House features the study, living room, sitting room, kitchen, dining room and one double. The study is furnished with basic office supplies and a printer. The living room includes a grand piano and a large television. The sitting room is well lit and, in the case it isn't, has a fireplace for cold nights.

Upper Floor

The top floor of G-House consists of well lit single rooms and one especially large triple master room, with its own built in bathroom.
